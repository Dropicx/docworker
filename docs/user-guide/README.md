## Introduction

HealthLingo is a medical document translation service that converts complex medical terminology into easy-to-understand language. Whether you have a doctor's letter, lab results, or radiology reports, our application helps you understand what they mean.

### Who is this for?
- Patients wanting to understand their medical documents
- Family members helping loved ones with medical information
- Healthcare advocates assisting clients
- Anyone needing to understand medical terminology

### Key Benefits
- **Simple Language**: Complex medical terms explained in everyday words
- **Complete Privacy**: Your documents are never stored
- **Fast Results**: Get translations in seconds
- **No Registration**: Use immediately without creating an account

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge recommended)
2. Navigate to the HealthLingo website
3. You'll see the main upload interface - no login required!

### System Requirements

- **Browser**: Modern web browser (updated within last 2 years)
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection
- **File Size**: Documents up to 10MB
- **Formats**: PDF, JPG, JPEG, or PNG files

## How to Use

### Step 1: Prepare Your Document

Before uploading:
- Ensure your document is clear and readable
- For photos: Take in good lighting, avoid shadows
- For scans: Use at least 200 DPI resolution
- File must be under 10MB

### Step 2: Upload Your Document

1. **Click the Upload Area**: Click on the dotted upload box or drag your file onto it
2. **Select Your File**: Choose your medical document from your device
3. **Automatic Processing**: The upload starts automatically

![Upload Process]
- You'll see a progress indicator
- File validation happens instantly
- If there's an issue, you'll get a clear error message

### Step 3: Wait for Processing

After upload, the system will:
1. **Extract Text** (10-20 seconds)
   - OCR technology reads your document
   - Works with handwritten and printed text

2. **Translate to Simple Language** (20-40 seconds)
   - AI analyzes medical terminology
   - Converts to patient-friendly language

3. **Prepare Results** (5-10 seconds)
   - Formats the translation
   - Adds helpful structure and explanations

**Total Time**: Usually 30-60 seconds

## Understanding Results

### Translation Structure

Each translation follows a consistent format:

#### Summary Section
- Quick overview of what type of document it is
- Main reason for the medical visit/test
- General assessment (normal, needs attention, etc.)

#### Main Findings
- Primary diagnoses in simple terms
- What conditions were found or ruled out
- Severity and implications explained

#### Detailed Explanations
- Lab values with normal ranges
- What high/low values mean
- Technical terms translated

#### Next Steps
- Recommended follow-up actions
- Medications to take
- Lifestyle recommendations
- When to see a doctor

### Confidence Indicators

The system provides confidence scores:
- **High (80-100%)**: Very reliable translation
- **Medium (60-79%)**: Generally accurate, verify important details
- **Low (<60%)**: May need professional interpretation

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is this service free?**
A: The availability and pricing depend on your organization's setup. Contact your administrator for details.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No, the service works without any registration or login.

**Q: Can I translate multiple documents?**
A: Yes, but one at a time. After each translation, you can start a new one.

**Q: How accurate are the translations?**
A: Generally 85-95% accurate for common medical documents. Complex or rare conditions may be less accurate.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What if my document won't upload?**
A: Check:
- File size (must be under 10MB)
- File format (PDF, JPG, PNG only)
- Internet connection
- Try a different browser

**Q: Why is processing taking long?**
A: Large documents or poor quality scans take longer. Normal processing is 30-60 seconds.

**Q: Can I save the translation?**
A: Use your browser's print function to save as PDF or copy the text to save elsewhere.

### Privacy Questions

**Q: Is my medical information safe?**
A: Yes, we don't store any data. Everything is deleted immediately after processing.

**Q: Who can see my documents?**
A: Only you. Documents are processed locally and never leave the secure server.

**Q: What about GDPR/HIPAA compliance?**
A: Fully GDPR compliant. HIPAA compliance depends on your organization's setup.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Upload Fails
**Problem**: "File validation failed" error
**Solutions**:
- Reduce file size (compress PDF or reduce image quality)
- Ensure file isn't corrupted
- Try a different file format
- Check file isn't password protected

#### Processing Stuck
**Problem**: Processing doesn't complete
**Solutions**:
- Wait up to 2 minutes
- Refresh the page and try again
- Check your internet connection
- Try a smaller or clearer document

#### Poor Translation Quality
**Problem**: Translation doesn't make sense
**Solutions**:
- Ensure document is clearly readable
- Check if document is in German
- Try photographing/scanning again with better quality
- Complex medical documents may need professional translation

#### Browser Issues
**Problem**: Page doesn't load or looks broken
**Solutions**:
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Disable browser extensions
- Update your browser

### Error Messages

| Error | Meaning | Solution |
|-------|---------|----------|
| "File too large" | Over 10MB limit | Compress or split document |
| "Invalid format" | Wrong file type | Convert to PDF or JPG |
| "Processing timeout" | Took too long | Try again with smaller file |
| "No text found" | OCR couldn't read | Improve scan quality |
| "Service unavailable" | System overloaded | Wait and try again |

### Getting Help

If problems persist:
1. Note the exact error message
2. Document what you were trying to do
3. Contact support with details
4. Alternative: Ask healthcare provider for explanation
